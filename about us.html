About

Chamba is a town in the Chamba district in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh.
According to the 2001 Indian census, Chamba has a population of 519,080 people.
Located at an altitude of 1,006 metres above mean sea level, the town is situated on the banks of the Ravi River, at its confluence with the Sal River.
